Who We Are

Just Education Matters CIC is a newly established company set up to tackle inequalities in education particularly in the area of school exclusions.

We support parents to support their children by providing training, advice, advocacy and support using a proactive approach to improve the educational outcomes for children

Our Start guide will signpost you to key information, services we provide and other key contacts to support you.
